Gain Control of Your Forms

The biggest challenge with WordPress forms until now has been that all form layouts are controlled by WordPress themes. This often leads to unpredictable form layouts. And worst, a lot of forms lack the ability to create custom landing pages. WPForms Form Pages solves this problem, so you don't have to settle for mediocre form layouts and can focus on what matters most: conversions! With Form Pages, you can instantly create a custom landing page for any of your WordPress forms by simply enabling the Form Pages Mode from your Form Settings. You can customize the colors, add your logo, choose between different high-converting form styles, pick a color scheme and that's it. Your distraction-free custom form landing page is ready to be shared.
